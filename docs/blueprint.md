# **App Name**: Krishione AI

## Core Features:

- Voice-Based AI Assistant: Process voice queries in Assamese, Hindi, and other local languages using Gemini and Whisper, providing crop advice, cattle treatment, form filling help as a tool, mandi rates, and government scheme information.
- AI Plant Disease Diagnosis: Diagnose plant diseases from uploaded images using Gemini Vision, providing solutions in the user's native language. Stores the image and diagnosis results.
- AI-Powered Form Filler: Autofill government forms (PAN, Kisan Credit, etc.) from voice input, streamlining the application process for farmers. Uses Gemini to extract relevant information.
- Personalized SMS Alerts: Send personalized weather and subsidy alerts via SMS in Assamese and Hindi, keeping farmers informed about critical updates.
- Local Marketplace: Enable farmers to buy and sell crops, cattle, or equipment locally through a marketplace feature within the app.
- AI Farm Reports: Generate AI-driven farm reports as PDF documents, offering insights into farm health, productivity, and potential issues.
- Farm Tracking Dashboard: Allow farmers to track their mood, stress level, cattle fertility, subsidies, and soil quality within the app, providing a holistic view of their farming operations.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant, earthy green (#6B8E23) symbolizing growth, nature, and prosperity in agriculture.
- Background color: A light, desaturated beige (#F5F5DC) providing a neutral, clean backdrop for the app's content.
- Accent color: A warm, analogous yellow (#FFD700) to highlight key actions and information, inspired by the color of grain and sunshine.
- Headline font: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for clear and modern headlines and subheadings. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for readable and accessible body text in various sections. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Use clear, representative icons sourced from Google's Material Design library. Represent concepts like weather, crops, cattle, and markets, etc.
- Design a tab-based layout for main sections (Voice Assistant, Disease Diagnosis, Marketplace, etc.). Design a card-based layout, emphasizing clear visual hierarchy, for data display and marketplace listings.
